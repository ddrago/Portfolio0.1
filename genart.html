Who am I?

Well, if we don't want to get into the phylosophical side of the question, I'm a PhD candidate focusing on Metaverse UX.

What do I do?

I study at the University of Glasgow for the Multimodal Interaction Group. I was lucky enough to be sponsored by Bell Labs to explore the use of sensory perception and activation to improve the UX of extended reality and to assess the benefits of multisensory interactions in the metaverse.
