Menu selection for cyclists: comparing voice, touchscreen and button controls

My Master's thesis focused on assessing and comparing suitable input methods for cycling AR Glasses. We evaluated voice input, touchscreen and button controls in a within-subjects field study. Our work provided insights into the design of interaction for cyclists by assessing the impact of three input methods on cycling performance, task performance, and several subjective measures.
